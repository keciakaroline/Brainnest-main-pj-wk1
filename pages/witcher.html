This page is about The Witcher

The Witcher is a 2007 action role-playing game, based on the fantasy novel series The Witcher.

The story takes place in a medieval fantasy world and follows Geralt of Rivia, one of a few traveling monster hunters who have supernatural powers, known as Witcher. The game's system of moral choices was noted for its time-delayed consequences and lack of black-and-white morality.
